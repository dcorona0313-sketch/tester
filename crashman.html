About Crash Man

Crash Man is a Robot Master who uses explosive Crash Bombs that stick to surfaces before detonating.

How to Beat Crash Man

Use Air Shooter to deal strong damage while avoiding his explosive attacks.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Keep moving to avoid explosive shots
  • Don’t stand near walls where bombs stick
  • Jump over incoming projectiles
  • Use Air Shooter for best damage
  • Crash Man fires bombs in a steady rhythm
  • Explosions happen shortly after impact
  • Attack between his throws
  • Stay calm and watch bomb timing
