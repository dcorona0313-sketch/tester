About Flash Man

Flash Man is a Robot Master who can stop time briefly using his Time Stopper ability, making him very dangerous in battle.

How to Beat Flash Man

Use Crash Bomber to deal strong damage while avoiding his time-freeze attack.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Watch for Time Stopper activation
  • Stay moving to avoid freezing traps
  • Keep distance when he charges energy
  • Use Crash Bomber for best damage
  • Flash Man attacks in short bursts
  • Attack right after time resumes
  • Don’t waste movement during freeze
  • Learn timing to avoid damage spikes
