About Metal Man

Metal Man is a Robot Master from Mega Man 2. His Metal Blade is fast and powerful.

How to Beat Metal Man

He is weak to his own Metal Blade weapon.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Best Time to Attack: Shoot while he walks.
  • Conveyor Belt: Easier when moving away from him.
  • Jump Trick: Let him jump over you.
  • Weakness: METAL BLADE.
  • Pattern: Throw, jump, repeat.
  • Best First Boss: Very useful weapon.
  • Jump Strategy: Jump over blades.
  • Stage Tip: Run before big jumps.
