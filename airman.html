About Air Man

Air Man is a Robot Master who uses powerful tornado attacks to push enemies away and control the battlefield.

How to Beat Air Man

Use Leaf Shield to block wind attacks and strike during his attack pauses.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Watch tornado pushback carefully
  • Stay grounded to avoid being launched
  • Use Leaf Shield for defense and damage
  • Keep mid-range distance
  • Air Man fires multiple tornado bursts
  • Attack only during his pause moments
  • Control movement instead of rushing
  • Don’t panic jump into wind attacks
