About Heat Man

Heat Man is a Robot Master who transforms into a fireball and dashes across the screen with intense speed.

How to Beat Heat Man

Use Bubble Lead to deal heavy damage while avoiding his fire dash attack.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Jump over his fire dash attack
  • Attack right after he stops moving
  • Stay calm and time your jumps
  • Use Bubble Lead for best results
  • Heat Man charges before dashing
  • His attacks follow a predictable rhythm
  • Don’t stand too close during charge
  • Learn his timing to avoid damage
