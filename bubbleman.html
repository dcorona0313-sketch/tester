About Bubble Man

Bubble Man fights underwater and uses Bubble Lead attacks that move along the ground.

How to Beat Bubble Man

Bubble Man is weak to the Metal Blade. Stay mobile and avoid his jumping attacks.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Watch Movement: He jumps often underwater.
  • Avoid Bubbles: Stay above ground attacks.
  • Keep Distance: Don’t get cornered.
  • Weakness: METAL BLADE.
  • Pattern: Jump + shoot bubbles.
  • Low Gravity: Jumps are slower underwater.
  • Timing: Attack after he lands.
  • Stage Tip: Manage oxygen sections.
