About Quick Man

Quick Man is one of the fastest Robot Masters in Mega Man 2, attacking with Quick Boomerangs and rapid movement.

How to Beat Quick Man

Use the Time Stopper to freeze him and deal massive damage safely.

Tips to Beat the Boss

  • Quick Man moves extremely fast
  • Use Time Stopper early in the fight
  • Stay alert for sudden direction changes
  • Keep distance to react properly
  • He throws boomerangs in quick bursts
  • Attack when he slows down briefly
  • Avoid getting cornered
  • Stay calm and time your jumps carefully
